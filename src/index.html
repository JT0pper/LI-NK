Rules

The goal is to find the next word in the "LI-NK" by guessing a word that would come after it

There are no proper nouns nor any combinations with single letters such as "a" then "lot"

If you get stuck, click on the hint button to get the first letter of the next box

Example

Front

Porch

Light

Bulb

Good luck, have fun, and remember, LI-NK is a word game
